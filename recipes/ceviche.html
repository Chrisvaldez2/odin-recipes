Ceviche Recipe

Description

Ceviche is a Mexican-inspired seafood dish that combines fresh shrimp or fish with acidity of the lemon

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Lime
  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Purple Onion
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Cut shrimp into half an inch pieces
  2. Before cutting the vegetables, squeeze lemon and add juice to the shrimp and let marinate
  3. Cut cucumbers and tomatoes into small cubes, add them to the shrimp
  4. Cut onion into miniscule pieces and add to the shrimp container
  5. Mix all the ingredients together and season to taste
  6. Cut cilantro and garnish as the last step before serving
