Tuna Sandwhich Recipe

Description

Golden-brown, buttery bread toasted to perfection with a generous filling of tuna salad

Ingredients

  • Artisan Sandwhich Bread
  • Tuna
  • Corn
  • Mayonaise
  • Pickle Juice
  • Celery

Steps

  1. Open your tuna can and empty the contents into a bowl
  2. Mix in your corn, chopped celery and pickle juice
  3. Add a few tablespoons of mayonaise
  4. Season to taste and mix until well combined
